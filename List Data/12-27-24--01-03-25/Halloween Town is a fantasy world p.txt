Halloween Town is a fantasy world populated by various monsters and supernatural beings. Jack Skellington, the well-respected Silent King, leads the town in organizing the annual Halloween celebrations. This year, however, Jack grows tired of the same routine and longs for something new. Upon discovering trees containing doors to other holiday-themed worlds, Jack stumbles through the one leading to Christmas Town and is fascinated by the unfamiliar holiday.
